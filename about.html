About Us

Hello there! My name is Vanesa and I am a busy mother of two beautiful children. I have been struggling all my life with weight loss and weight management. I started my keto lifestyle in 2017, and it helped me reach a healthy weight. I would like to share my experience with everyone that needs a change in their life, but can't do a traditional diet. The Keto journey is not an magic solution, but it's a lifestyle that can help you reach your goals and maintain it in the long-term.
